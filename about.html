Nurturing great ideas
since 1869

Luzzatto and Luzzatto is one of the leading intellectual property firms in Israel. Since 1869, the firm has helped companies, inventors, and entrepreneurs protect their intellectual property and fulfill the potential of their innovation Our firm brings five generations of expertise to cutting-edge start-ups and established Fortune 500 companies alike, both in the Start-up Nation and worldwide.

Many of our clients have been faithful to the firm for decades, a reflection of our deep understanding of their evolving intellectual property needs and the trust they place in our multi-disciplinary experience, technological knowledge, and international perspectives.We serve a wide range of clients seeking our services in IP strategy, patents, trademarks, and designs, and have extensive experience across diverse industries, including pharmaceuticals, medical devices, life sciences, foodtech, fashion and cosmetics, nanotechnology, machine learning and AI, robotics, software, cybertech, and more.

Luzzatto and Luzzatto combines a family tradition of legal practice with a state-of-the-art work environment that leverages modern technologies to ensure efficient and transparent work processes.

Our experienced team of IP professionals includes specialists with advanced degrees in a wide range of technical backgrounds and disciplines and a dedicated support team focused on handling the essential administrative aspects of patent work. (ADD LINKS TO TEAM)

We are committed to staying at the cutting edge of our profession which means being always up to date and striving to achieve the highest possible quality in all areas of our work. We are ISO 9001-2000 certified as a part of our commitment to maintaining the highest governance standards. Luzzatto and Luzzatto is a family-run business that has been faithfully passed down for five consecutive generations, with each generation continuing to uphold our original values. Dr. Kfir Luzzatto, senior partner and president of The Luzzatto Group, is the fourth generation of his family to work in intellectual property. His wife, Dr. Esther Luzzatto, is the Managing Partner and CEO of The Luzzatto Group. The fifth generation of the family is actively involved in the management of the Group - Lilach Luzzatto Shukrun is a Senior Partner, Michal Luzzatto is the managing partner of the Luzzatto Law Firm and Head of Trademarks at Luzzatto and Luzzatto, and Tamar Luzzatto is Head of Business Development, Marketing, and Innovation of the Group (ADD LINKS).

The Luzzatto Group offers clients a range of complementary services to maximize the value of their IP. Luzzatto and Luzzatto Patent Attorneys and The Luzzatto Law Firm are part of The Luzzatto Group’s IP division. The Business Division of the Group includes; Luzzatto Consulting, which provides professional, technical, and scientific consulting; Luzzatto Software, which develops software for the Group’s needs; Luzzatto BizDev, which creates business opportunities for the Group and, in some cases, for the Group’s clients - providing opportunities for growth.

Our Vision
Sustaining Innovation

At Luzzatto and Luzzatto, we are dedicated to enabling the world’s most inventive individuals and companies to innovate with confidence, knowing their ideas can fulfill their commercial and social potential.

For more than 150 years, our mission has been to empower innovation worldwide by protecting transformational ideas and inventors.

Our Values

Expertise
